# Bucket personalization — CarterCo's own outbound

Becc Holland's "6 buckets of personalization" applied to CarterCo's own
LinkedIn outbound. The personalized opening LINE of the video DM is generated by
a **sequential waterfall** that cascades through the buckets best-first and stops
at the first one with a genuinely *connected* line.

Code: `_shared/bucket-hook.ts` (production, Deno) · `scripts/bucket-hooks/*.py`
(iteration harnesses) · edge fn `enrich-buckets` · fires on CarterCo
connection-accept from `sendpilot-webhook`.

## The waterfall

```
accept
 └─ scrape B1 posts        → connected line worth sending?  YES → STOP
 └─ scrape B2 engaged      (only the new stuff)             YES → STOP
 └─ scrape B3 self-written (one profile call → also B5)     YES → STOP
 └─        B5 background   (reuse the profile call)         YES → STOP
 └─ scrape B6 company deep (site + careers + news)          YES → STOP
 └─ floor: honest website line (NEVER fake personalization)
```

- **Cheapest / highest-priority first, stop early.** Most leads stop at B1 or B3,
  so the profile/company scrapes never run for them.
- **Only scrape what isn't already scraped.** `hook_buckets_tried` (text col on
  `outreach_pipeline`) records which buckets were tried.
- **Time axis:** because the tried-state persists, a *floored* lead can resume
  deeper on a LATER touch (re-scrape may surface a fresh post / new company
  event). The bucket waterfall and the engagement/time waterfall are the **same
  mechanism**.

DB columns on `outreach_pipeline`: `personalized_hook`, `hook_bucket`,
`hook_trace`, `hook_lang`, `hook_buckets_tried`, `hook_generated_at`. The hook is
baked into `rendered_message` at render-ready (`sendspark-webhook`); the
`/outreach` approval card shows the bucket chip + trace; the line is editable
there (binding-approved-text). Null hook → static website fallback.

## The strict bar (what qualifies as a signal)

A bucket only "wins" if it has a **concrete particular** the prospect would read
and feel was written for *them*:

- ✅ a real post/topic, a specific build/achievement, a real career move, a named
  cert, a specific line they wrote about themselves, a real company event.
- ❌ **bare title + tenure** ("11 år som Sales Director") — this is role-mad-libs
  with a number; it connects to nothing specific. Reject it and cascade.
- ❌ anything that fails the **DELETE TEST** (see below).

> We'd rather floor (plain honest line) than send fake-specific. Floor rate going
> up is fine if every line that *is* there is real.

## The voice (locked over many operator iterations)

The line is the opening of a video DM. It must be:

1. **CONNECTED — the delete test.** It bridges to CarterCo's value (inbound leads
   cooling, slow/missing response, follow-up slipping). *If you delete the line
   and the message still makes sense, it FAILS* — it was decorative.
2. **Peer, never a judge (not "bedrevidende").** Credit their experience, defer
   to it, pose the problem as something they already know. BANNED grading words:
   imponerende, respekt for, sjældent man ser, stærkt, flot, godt gjort, dygtig.
3. **Not reciting their facts back** ("Så du byggede X"). Reference, don't narrate.
4. **LinkedIn-light + DANISH-MODEST (Jante).** Warmer/shorter than a cold email,
   a genuine light question is fine. Understated and tentative — too confident
   reads as cocky. Hedge: "jeg gætter på...", "jeg tænker...", "det er vel...",
   "mon ikke...", "du kender det bedre end mig". Defer DOWN, never up.
5. **Vary the move** — no single phrase ("jeg gætter på", "du ved bedre end")
   should dominate. (Per-lead independent generation tends to cluster on one
   phrase; a rotation seed forces spread.)
6. **Language-matched** — Danish for Danish prospects, their language only if they
   clearly post in another (e.g. Cristian → Spanish).
7. Ends in a colon (:) leading into the video. Sonnet (`claude-sonnet-4-6`) writes
   the line; the cascade/judge can run on Haiku.

**Floor line:** `Jeg var lige inde på jeres side og optog en kort video om én
ting, jeg tror I mister lidt værdi på:`

## Cost

~1 cent/profile for the posts/profile actors; Firecrawl pennies/page; one Sonnet
call per bucket attempted (most leads: 1–2 calls). At CarterCo's volume (tens of
accepts/month) it's a few dollars/month. Apify is on a paid plan ($29/mo cap).
